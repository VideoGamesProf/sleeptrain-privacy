Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

Welcome to Sleep Train. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. Sleep Train is designed to be a relaxing experience, not a data-harvesting tool. We collect the following types of information:

  • Gameplay Data: Progress, high scores, and unlocked features. This is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (e.g., session length, crash reports) to help us improve the game's performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Sleep Train experience. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Save your progress and preferences.
  • Analyze game performance to fix bugs and optimize the experience.
  • Respond to your customer service requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your local gameplay data is stored securely on your device. Any anonymous analytics transmitted to our servers are encrypted during transit and stored on secure servers. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may use trusted third-party services (like game engine analytics) that assist us in operating our game, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Children's Privacy

Sleep Train is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such data immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please reach out to us:
