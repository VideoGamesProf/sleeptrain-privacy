Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Welcome to Sleep Train — a gamified companion for behavioral infant sleep training. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. The simplest way to do that is to not collect any data at all.

1. Data Collection

Sleep Train does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We believe your privacy is non-negotiable.

  • No account or registration is required to use the app.
  • No personal information — no email, name, phone number, or identifiers are ever collected.
  • No analytics or tracking — we do not use any analytics services, crash reporters, or usage trackers.
  • No advertising — the app contains no ads and no ad-related tracking.
  • No cookies or web beacons of any kind.

2. On-Device Data

Sleep Train stores session data — such as timer durations and your preferences — locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party.
  • Is automatically removed if you uninstall the app.
  • Can be cleared at any time through your device settings.

3. Internet Usage

The app functions fully offline. No network requests carrying personal data are made at any time. No information is transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

Sleep Train does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks embedded in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Sleep Train is a tool designed for parents and guardians. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or any user of any age. Because no data is collected at all, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated \"Last Updated\" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you may reach us through the app's listing page on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
