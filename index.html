Sleep Train

Privacy Policy

Last updated — February 25, 2026

Overview

Sleep Train is a gamified companion for behavioral infant sleep training. We are committed to protecting your privacy — and the simplest way to do that is to not collect any data at all.

Data Collection

Sleep Train does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account or registration required
  • No email, name, or personal information collected
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No third-party advertising
  • No cookies or web beacons

On-Device Data

Sleep Train stores session data (timer durations and preferences) locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is automatically removed if you uninstall the app
  • Can be cleared at any time through your device settings

Internet Usage

The app functions fully offline. No personal data is transmitted at any time.

Children's Privacy

Sleep Train is a tool for parents and guardians. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may reach us through the app's listing page on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
